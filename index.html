About

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering at West Virginia University , supervised by Dr. Xin Li.

My research interests are computer vision, deep learning, and image analysis. My current research is focused on object re-identification and machine learning theory.
